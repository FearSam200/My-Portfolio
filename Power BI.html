Power BI Projects

This project is a visualization on commercial taxi trips, where we explore the dynamics and trends within the taxi transportation industry. Commercial taxi services play a vital role in urban mobility, providing convenient and accessible transportation options for passengers in cities around the world.


Recommendations
Integrate data from various sources, including taxi dispatch systems, GPS tracking devices, and customer feedback platforms, to gain a comprehensive understanding of taxi operations. Develop predictive models to forecast passenger demand for taxi services based on historical data, event calendars, and external factors such as weather conditions and traffic patterns.

This project is a visualization on 1853 stockolm cholera outbreak. This outbreak was one of the most severe cholera epidemics in Sweden's history, with widespread illness, mortality, and societal disruption. The outbreak in Stockholm occurred against the backdrop of inadequate sanitation, poor living conditions, and limited access to clean water, factors that contributed to the rapid spread of the disease.


Recommendations
Assessing the public health response to the Stockholm cholera outbreak, including measures implemented to control the spread of the disease, treat affected individuals, and improve sanitation and hygiene practices. Exploring the socioeconomic determinants that contributed to the vulnerability of certain communities to cholera during the outbreak.
